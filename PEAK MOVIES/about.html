About PEAK

PEAK is your go to streaming service for all things entertainment. With a vast library of movies, TV shows, documentaries, and original content, we cater to every taste and preference. Our user friendly interface and personalized recommendations make it easy to find what you love. Join us today and experience the future of streaming.

The owners Håkon, Liam and Kevin are big supporters of the film industry and are committed to providing a platform for diverse voices and stories. Even if it is a universal studio production or a small independent film that someone made with their friends, we see passion in every project. We believe that dreams are something that can never be killed and we want to make yours come true.

One fourth of our revenue each year goes to supporting independent filmmakers, promoting innovative storytelling, funding new projects that might not otherwise get made, and funding college tuition to film school. Because we believe that everyone has a story and it deserves to be told. Film has always been a powerful form of art that allows people to share ideas, emotions, and perspectives with the world. By investing in creators we hope to help the next generation of filmmakers bring their visions to life.

When we started this website we were sixteen years old and had spent most of our lives exploring technology and software development. We loved building websites and applications, but we also loved film and storytelling. That passion led us to experiment with directing and production. What started as a creative experiment quickly became something much bigger than we expected.

During that time we created projects like The Shawshank Redemption and Breaking Bad. The experience taught us how powerful storytelling can be and how much work goes into bringing a story from an idea to the screen. Through that process we developed an even deeper respect for directors, writers, editors, actors, and everyone who works behind the scenes in film production.

After winning several awards including Oscars and Emmys we realized something important. There were thousands of talented creators around the world who did not always have the opportunity to share their work with large audiences. Many independent filmmakers create incredible stories but struggle to find a place where their films can be seen.

That realization inspired the creation of PEAK. We wanted to build a platform where directors like ourselves could showcase their work and reach viewers who truly appreciate creativity and storytelling. Our vision was simple. Create a space where art could thrive and where filmmakers of all backgrounds could share their ideas without limits.

Today PEAK continues to grow as a home for storytellers and audiences. We are constantly adding new films, new series, and new voices to the platform. Some creators are experienced professionals while others are sharing their very first project. What matters most to us is the passion behind the work.

The art you create deserves to be seen. Every filmmaker has a unique voice and every story has the power to inspire someone watching it. By supporting creators and building a global community of viewers we hope to make PEAK a place where imagination can thrive and where the future of film can continue to grow.
